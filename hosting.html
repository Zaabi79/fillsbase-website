Frequently Asked Questions

Questions about our shared hosting plans? We have the answers.

What is Shared Hosting?

  • Shared hosting means your website is hosted on a server alongside other sites, sharing resources such as CPU and RAM. It is the most affordable and beginner-friendly hosting option — perfect for small businesses, blogs and portfolios. At Fillsbase, our shared plans include cPanel, free SSL, unlimited email and daily backups.

Does Shared Web Hosting also include Email Hosting?

  • Yes! All Fillsbase shared hosting plans include professional email hosting. You can create custom email addresses with your domain (e.g. you@yourbusiness.com), set up redirects, auto-replies and access your mail via webmail or any email client such as Outlook or Gmail.

Can I upgrade my hosting plan later?

  • Absolutely. You can upgrade from Business SSD to Pro SSD — or migrate to a VPS or dedicated server — at any time directly from your client portal. Upgrades are seamless with no service interruption, and you only pay the pro-rated difference for the remaining billing period.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

  • Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all shared hosting plans. If you are not fully satisfied within the first 30 days, contact our support team and we will refund your payment — no questions asked.
