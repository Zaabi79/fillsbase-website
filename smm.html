FAQ — Social Media Marketing

How many posts per week do you create?

  • Depending on your plan, we create 3 to 7 posts per week per platform. We also include Stories/Reels content. Posting frequency is agreed in advance and documented in your monthly content calendar.

Do you need access to our accounts?

  • Yes — we are added as a page manager or admin (never as the account owner). You always retain full ownership. We use Meta Business Suite and LinkedIn Campaign Manager to manage everything securely.

Can you manage paid ads in addition to organic?

  • Yes. Our SMM plans can include paid social ad management (Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads). We recommend combining organic and paid for maximum impact — organic builds trust, paid accelerates reach.
