FAQ — Questions about AI Automation

What clients ask before their first automation project.

Do I need to replace my existing software?

  • No. We build automation layers on top of your existing tools via APIs and webhooks. Your team keeps using the same software — they just stop doing the repetitive parts manually.

How is this different from Zapier or Make?

  • Zapier and Make are great for simple "if this, then that" rules. We handle the complex, messy cases — unstructured data, conditional logic, AI decision-making, exception handling and high-volume pipelines that no-code tools struggle to handle at scale.

How quickly can I see results?

  • Most clients have their first automation live within 2 to 3 weeks. We start with the highest-impact, lowest-risk workflow so you see clear ROI before moving on to more complex pipelines.

What happens if the automation makes a mistake?

  • Every automation we build includes confidence thresholds, error logging and human escalation paths. Low-confidence cases are flagged for human review rather than handled incorrectly — you always have a safety net.
