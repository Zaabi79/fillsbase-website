FAQ — Content Writing

Do you use AI to write the content?

  • Our content is written by human writers who may use AI tools for research and ideation — but every piece is written, edited and fact-checked by a human. We never deliver raw AI-generated text. The result reads naturally and passes plagiarism checks.

Can you match our brand voice?

  • Yes. We start every engagement with a brand voice document — your tone, vocabulary, style preferences and things to avoid. After the first 2–3 pieces, our writers know your voice well enough to produce consistently on-brand content.

How quickly can you deliver?

  • Standard turnaround is 3 to 5 business days per article. We offer express delivery (1 to 2 days) for an additional fee. For recurring contracts, we build a content pipeline so you always have articles ready before you need them.
