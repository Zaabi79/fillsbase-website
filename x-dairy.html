Milk Parlor Management

Milk Parlor Management helps dairy farms efficiently manage daily milk collection and production records from a single system. Farmers can record milk collected from each cow, track milking sessions, and monitor cow-wise milk yield in an organized way. The module ensures accurate record keeping by storing all milk collection data digitally, reducing manual errors and saving time. It also allows farmers to monitor production trends and evaluate herd performance over time. By maintaining clear and structured milk records, the system supports better decision-making, improves farm productivity, and helps dairy farms manage their milk operations more effectively and reliably.

View detailed analytics of milk production including daily, weekly, and monthly reports. Analyze cow performance, milk yield trends, and production efficiency to improve farm productivity.

Feed Management

Feed Management is a complete module designed to help dairy farmers efficiently manage and monitor all feed-related activities on the farm. It enables accurate tracking of feed allocation, consumption, and scheduling to ensure every animal receives the right nutrition at the right time. With Feed Analytics, farmers gain deep insights into feed usage patterns and costs, helping reduce waste and improve efficiency. TMR Formulas allow farmers to create balanced ration plans tailored to different animal groups, while the Cow Feed feature ensures feed is properly allocated to individual cows or groups. The Feeding Schedule module organizes daily feeding routines, reducing the chances of missed or irregular feedings. Together, these tools support healthier herd growth, better milk production, and more cost-effective farm operations.

Feed Analytics

Analyze feed usage, costs, and consumption trends across the farm. Gain insights into feeding efficiency to minimize waste and optimize nutrition management for better productivity.

TMR Formulas

Cow Feed

Feeding Schedule

Frequently Asked Questions — FAQ

What Is X-Dairy?
X-Dairy is an AI-powered, all-in-one dairy farm management system built on Laravel. It lets farmers manage milk production, cow records, health, reproduction, feeding, finance, and employees from a single smart dashboard.
Who Can Use X-Dairy?
X-Dairy is suitable for small, medium, and large dairy farms, as well as veterinarians, farm managers, and agribusiness owners looking to digitize and automate operations.
What Modules Are Included In X-Dairy?
X-Dairy includes 10+ modules: Milk Parlor, Cow Management, Health, Reproduction, Supplier, Feed, Finance, Stock, Employee, Catalog, Configuration, and Farm Tools & Calculators.
Does X-Dairy Support Real-Time Analytics?
Yes. X-Dairy provides real-time AI analytics for milk production, cow health, finances, and all key farm metrics from a live dashboard.
Can I Customize The System For My Farm?
Yes. You receive the full Laravel source code. Settings, categories, user roles, and workflows are all configurable and extendable.
How Do I Get Started With X-Dairy?
Choose your package → purchase → install on your server → configure farm data → start managing operations. Professional and Enterprise plans include full installation and server setup.
Is My Data Secure?
Yes. X-Dairy uses role-based access control, SSL encryption, and WAF firewall protection. Daily backups are included on the Enterprise plan.
Can I Track Individual Cow Records?
Yes. Each cow has a detailed Animal Profile with health records, milk production history, reproduction status, feed allocation, and more — all in one place.
Does The System Send Alerts Or Reminders?
Yes. X-Dairy sends AI-driven automated alerts for vaccinations, heat detection cycles, calving dates, medication schedules, and other critical farm events.
Is X-Dairy Available In Offline Mode?
Offline mode is available on Professional and Enterprise plans only. The Basic plan supports online mode exclusively.
Can I Manage Farm Finances With X-Dairy?
Yes. The Finance module covers financial analysis, total assets, current and non-current assets, expense tracking, and full farm accounting reports.
Does X-Dairy Provide Reports And Exports?
Yes. All modules support detailed reporting with PDF and Excel export options for compliance, auditing, and business planning purposes.
Is Technical Support Included?
Yes, all plans include 6 months of technical support. Support can be renewed after 6 months for $50 per renewal.
Do You Provide Installation And Server Setup?
Complete installation and server setup are included in the Professional and Enterprise plans. The Basic plan includes an installation guide only.
Can Multiple Users Access The System?
Yes. X-Dairy supports multi-user access with role-based permissions. You can assign specific access levels and user types to control what each team member can view or edit.
