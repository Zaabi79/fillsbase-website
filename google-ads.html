FAQ — Google Ads

What is the minimum budget to get started?

  • We recommend a minimum ad budget of $300–$500/month to collect enough data to optimize. Lower budgets can work for highly targeted local campaigns, but we will advise you honestly on what is achievable for your goals.

Do you charge a percentage of ad spend?

  • We charge a fixed monthly management fee — not a percentage of your ad spend. This means we are incentivized to maximize your results, not your budget. Ad spend goes directly to Google in your account.

How quickly will I see results?

  • Google Ads can generate leads within 24–48 hours of launch. The first 2–4 weeks are a learning phase during which we collect data and optimize. Most clients see solid, consistent results from week 3 or 4 onwards.
