FAQ — Common SaaS questions

Everything founders ask before starting their SaaS project.

What technology stack do you use to build SaaS?

  • We typically use Laravel or Node.js for the backend, React or Next.js for the frontend, and PostgreSQL or MySQL for the database. We choose the stack based on your product needs, your team's preferences and scaling requirements — not a one-size-fits-all approach.

How long does it take to build an MVP?

  • A well-scoped MVP typically takes 4 to 8 weeks. The more clearly your features are defined before we start, the faster we move. Our initial discovery sprint is designed to lock down scope and avoid costly mid-development changes.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?

  • Yes. All packages include a post-launch support period, and we offer monthly contracts for new feature development, bug fixes, performance monitoring and infrastructure management.

Will I own the source code?

  • Absolutely. After full payment, you receive complete ownership of the entire source code, assets and intellectual property. We hand over the full Git repository and all access — you're never locked in to us.
