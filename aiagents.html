FAQ — Still have questions?

Everything you need to know about AI automation and what we build.

What is an AI Agent?

  • An AI Agent is a software system powered by a large language model (LLM) that can perceive its environment, reason about goals and take actions — such as calling APIs, searching the web, writing code or sending messages — to accomplish tasks autonomously without constant human intervention.

How does AI automation differ from traditional automation (RPA)?

  • Traditional RPA follows rigid rules and breaks when processes change. AI agents understand natural language, intelligently handle exceptions and adapt to new situations — making them far more resilient and capable for complex, variable workflows.

What tools and platforms can AI agents integrate with?

  • Our agents integrate with virtually any platform that has an API — including WHMCS, Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, WhatsApp, Google Workspace, Shopify, MySQL databases and custom internal systems.

Is my data safe with AI agents?

  • Yes. We deploy agents following enterprise-grade security practices — with role-based permissions, encrypted credentials, audit logs, and the ability to run entirely on-premises or in your private cloud environment so your data never leaves your infrastructure.
