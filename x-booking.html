Hotel Booking

The Hotel module lets property owners and travel agencies list, manage, and sell hotel rooms through a fully automated booking engine. Guests can search by location, dates, and preferences, view real-time availability, compare room types, and complete reservations in seconds. Hoteliers get a powerful back-office to manage inventory, set dynamic pricing rules, block dates, and track every reservation. Automated confirmation emails, invoice generation, and payment tracking eliminate manual follow-up, while the AI occupancy engine forecasts demand and suggests pricing adjustments to maximize revenue across every season and property.

Booking Analytics

View daily, weekly, and monthly booking reports. Analyze occupancy rates, revenue per room, and guest demographics to optimize pricing and availability.

Availability Calendar

Visual drag-and-drop calendar to block dates, manage seasonal rates, and monitor real-time room availability across all property types.

Dynamic Pricing

Set rules-based pricing based on season, occupancy level, and advance booking window to automatically maximize revenue per available room.

Room Management

Add and manage room types with photos, amenities, bed configuration, and per-night pricing. Set different rates for weekdays and weekends.

Invoice & Payments

Auto-generate invoices on booking confirmation. Accept online payments via Stripe, PayPal, or bank transfer with full transaction history.

Reviews & Ratings

Collect and display verified guest reviews with star ratings. Respond to feedback and showcase top-rated properties to boost conversions.

Automated Emails

Send booking confirmations, check-in reminders, and post-stay review requests automatically using customizable email templates.

Tours & Activities

The Tours module enables travel operators to list, manage, and sell guided experiences effortlessly. From half-day city walks to multi-week adventures, operators can define itineraries, availability windows, group size limits, pricing tiers, and included services. Customers browse featured tours by destination, duration, and category, then book and pay instantly. The AI recommendation engine surfaces the most relevant tours based on traveller history and trending destinations. Automated booking confirmations, guide assignment notifications, and post-trip review requests keep operations smooth and conversion rates high.

Itinerary Builder

Create detailed day-by-day itineraries with stops, activities, meals, and accommodation for each tour package.

Group Management

Set minimum and maximum group sizes, manage waitlists, and automatically close bookings when capacity is reached.

Discount & Promo

Create early-bird discounts, group rates, coupon codes, and last-minute deals to maximize tour occupancy.

Guide Assignment

Assign certified guides to tours, track their schedules, and send automated briefing notifications before each departure.

Destination Manager

Manage destination pages with photos, highlights, and linked tours. Top-destination rankings auto-update from booking data.

Tour Analytics

Analyze booking volumes, revenue per tour, conversion rates, and seasonal trends to optimize your catalog and marketing spend.

Car Rental

The Car Rental module gives fleet operators a complete digital solution to list, manage, and rent out vehicles of any type. Each vehicle listing includes full specs — make, model, seats, fuel type, transmission, and photos — alongside daily pricing and location details. Customers filter by city, vehicle type, and date range to find the perfect car instantly. The system handles booking confirmations, driver option add-ons, insurance tier selection, and payment processing automatically. Fleet managers gain a unified view of availability, utilization rates, maintenance schedules, and revenue per vehicle — all from one powerful dashboard.

Fleet Management

Add unlimited vehicles with photos, specs, and condition notes. Track each car's booking history, mileage, and service schedule.

Pickup & Drop-off

Define multiple pickup and return locations. Support airport transfers, city-centre pickups, and one-way rentals with different pricing.

Insurance & Add-ons

Offer optional extras such as insurance tiers, GPS units, child seats, and fuel plans as paid add-ons at the checkout stage.

Event Booking

The Event module empowers organizers to create, publish, and sell tickets to any type of event — concerts, corporate conferences, cultural festivals, workshops, and private experiences. Each listing includes a rich description, venue map, schedule, speaker profiles, and multiple ticket categories with independent pricing. Attendees can browse upcoming events, select seats or ticket tiers, and pay securely in any supported currency. The AI-driven attendance predictor helps organizers anticipate demand, while automated reminders, QR code tickets, and check-in tools make day-of logistics effortless.

Ticket Tiers

Create General Admission, VIP, and Early Bird ticket categories with individual pricing, quotas, and sale windows for each event.

QR Code Check-in

Generate unique QR code tickets for every booking. Use the mobile check-in app to scan and validate tickets instantly at the venue entrance.

Attendance Analytics

Track ticket sales in real time, monitor occupancy against capacity, and analyze revenue by ticket tier and sales channel.

Manage flight schedules, seat classes, and charter inventory from one dashboard. Support one-way, round-trip, and multi-city searches with real-time seat selection, baggage add-ons, and meal preferences. The system handles booking confirmations, boarding pass generation, and automated flight status notifications so passengers are always informed.

Flight Schedules

Baggage & Add-ons

Sell baggage allowance, seat upgrades, and meal selections as paid extras at checkout.

List yachts, sailboats, and cruise vessels with capacity, speed, and length specifications. Manage charter durations, captain options, departure ports, and seasonal pricing for every vessel. Customers compare vessels by specs and photos, select their dates and crew preferences, and book instantly with secure online payment.

Vessel Listings

Captain & Crew

Set per-night or per-day pricing, define amenities and house rules, block unavailable dates, and upload photo galleries for each listing. Guests browse and filter by location, size, and amenities, then book and pay instantly. Automated check-in instructions and post-stay reviews are sent without any manual effort.

Property Listings

Availability Blocks

Handle visa applications end-to-end through a structured digital workflow. Agents upload document checklists per destination country, set processing fees and timelines, and manage each application through the pipeline. Applicants receive automated status updates at every stage — from submission and review to approval and passport return. The system keeps a complete audit trail and supports bulk processing for group visa applications.

Document Checklist

Status Tracking

Frequently Asked Questions — FAQ

What Is X Booking?
X Booking is an AI-powered, all-in-one travel and booking platform built on Laravel. It lets businesses manage Hotel, Tours, Space, Car, Event, Flight, Boat, and Visa bookings from a single smart dashboard.
Who Can Use X Booking?
X Booking is suitable for travel agencies, hotels, tour operators, car rental companies, event organizers, boat charter businesses, and any OTA looking to digitize and automate their booking operations.
What Booking Modules Are Included?
X Booking includes 9+ modules: Hotel, Tours & Activities, Space Rental, Car Rental, Event Booking, Flight Booking, Boat Charter, Visa Processing, and Admin & Configuration.
Does X Booking Support Multiple Currencies?
Yes. X Booking supports multiple currencies with automatic exchange rate conversion. Customers can browse and pay in their preferred currency across all booking types.
Can I Customize The Platform For My Brand?
Yes. You receive the full Laravel source code. Themes, colors, languages, payment methods, and all booking workflows are fully configurable and extendable.
How Do I Get Started With X Booking?
Choose your package → purchase → install on your server → add your listings → start accepting bookings. Professional and Enterprise plans include full installation and server setup.
What Payment Gateways Are Supported?
X Booking supports Stripe, PayPal, bank transfer, and regional payment gateways. All payments are processed securely with automatic invoice generation and refund management.
Does The System Send Automated Emails?
Yes. X Booking sends automated booking confirmations, payment receipts, check-in reminders, review requests, and cancellation notices using fully customizable email templates.
Is X Booking Available In Multiple Languages?
Yes. The platform supports multiple frontend and backend languages. You can add new language files easily to serve international travellers in their native language.
Can I Manage Finance And Revenue Reports?
Yes. The admin dashboard includes detailed revenue reports, booking statistics, payment history, payout management, and exportable PDF and Excel reports across all modules.
Is Technical Support Included?
Yes, all plans include 6 months of technical support. Support can be renewed after 6 months for AED 50 per renewal.
Can Multiple Vendors List On The Platform?
Yes. X Booking supports multi-vendor operation. Each vendor gets their own dashboard to manage listings, bookings, and payouts while the platform owner controls commission rates and approvals.
